Record of KOHLI

He was born in Delhi, India on November 5, 1988. Virat is the first player in ICC cricket history to win all 3 ICC awards in a single year- ICC , ODI player of the year, ICC Test player of the year and ICC Player of the year award in 2018. He is rated as one of the best batsmen in the world.

Virat Kohli was born on 5 November 1988 in Delhi into a Punjabi Hindu family. His father, Prem Kohli, worked as a criminal lawyer and his mother, Saroj Kohli, served as a housewife. He has an older brother, Vikas, and an older sister, Bhawna.

One word that is often used to describe Virat Kohli is "Maestro." This term signifies his mastery of the game of cricket, particularly his extraordinary batting skills and ability to dominate bowlers across all formats of the sport..

A Pillar of Strength and Resilience: Kohli's ability to overcome challenges and setbacks has made him a pillar of strength and resilience for the youth of India. He teaches them the importance of staying positive, learning from mistakes, and never giving up on their goals.
